Who We Are

Vista Labs is a third-party testing and product certification service provider strategically located in Orange County. Our goal is to certify your products and ensure their compliance with regulatory standards in order to help you access world-wide markets.

Our company was founded in 2016 by a senior electronics and compliance engineer and graduate computer scientist with the vision of providing world class service in the testing and compliance space. Through dedication, passion, and determination, Vista Compliance Laboratories was born.

Vista Labs is your testing and product certification service provider, providing services in the electrical and telecommunication industries.



What We Do

We provide full compliance testing for prototype and finished electrical and electronic products. We also help customers obtain regulatory certifications recognized both domestically and internationally.

Our comprehensive services include safety testing, EMC emission and susceptibility testing, RF and wireless testing (including Bluetooth, SAR and DFS), and telecommunications testing (including TTE).

We offer compliance testing services in ISO 17025 accredited laboratories as well as product certification service by ISO 17065 accredited certification body.
